How the Debt Grows

Each year we spend money. In 2015 we spent $3,324,564,245.15

Each year we collect taxes. In 2015 we collected $2,450,040,304.59

What we didn't collect in taxes is our national debt. In 2015 the national debt rose by $934,342,566,385.12
